📜 The Codex of Deception

🎯 Goal

This is a card game like UNO — but with hidden roles. Good players try to survive and empty their hand. Evil players try to deceive and destroy. Use cards + special abilities to outsmart the enemy faction.

🃏 How Cards Work

  • Cards have a number (1–10) and a color (Red, Blue, Green, Yellow).
  • On your turn: play a card that matches the color OR number of the top discard card.
  • If you can't play, draw one card from the deck (1 draw per turn max).
  • Curse Cards ☠️ — Drawing one forces your next turn to be skipped.
  • First good player to empty their hand wins — or the first team to eliminate all enemies.

⚡ Special Abilities — One-Time Powers

  • 🔮 The Mage — Sees all evil players from the start. Cannot tell anyone directly.
  • ⚔️ Jose — Strike: Choose a player. If evil → eliminated. If good → Jose loses 2 cards.
  • 🛡️ Carl — Shield: Protect any player from the next ability used against them.
  • 👑 Louise — Three powers: Corrupt Draw (+2 to target), False Vision (see alignment), Silence Curse (skip ability).
  • 🕷️ Nica — Hunt: Guess who the Mage is. Correct = Evil wins instantly.

🏆 Win Conditions

  • 🟦 Good Team Wins: Any good player empties their hand, OR all evil players are eliminated.
  • 🟥 Evil Team Wins: Nica correctly identifies the Mage, OR all good players are eliminated.

👥 The Roles

🔮 The Mage — Loyal Servant of Rina
Sees all evil players from the very start. Must stay hidden. If Nica identifies them, the evil team wins instantly.
⚔️ Jose — Loyal Servant of Rina
Can eliminate one player. Kills evil = great. Kills good = Jose loses 2 cards as punishment.
🛡️ Carl — Loyal Servant of Rina
Shields any one player from the next ability used against them. Shield expires after one use.
👑 Louise (The Malveraxis) — Shadow Court
Corrupt Draw, False Vision, Silence Curse. Three powerful abilities to disrupt good players.
🕷️ Nica — Shadow Court
Hunts the Mage. If she guesses correctly — evil team wins immediately.
