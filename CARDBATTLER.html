A Game of Cards & Hidden Shadows

📜 The Codex of Deception

🎯 Goal

This is a card game like UNO — but with hidden roles. Good players try to survive. Evil players try to destroy the Mage. Use cards + abilities to outsmart the enemy.

🃏 How Cards Work

  • Cards have a number (1–10) and a color (Red, Blue, Green, Yellow).
  • On your turn: play a card that matches the color OR number of the top card on the pile.
  • If you can't play, draw a card from the deck.
  • Some cards are Curse Cards ☠️ — drawing them forces you to skip a turn or lose a card.
  • First player to empty their hand wins — OR if a team's win condition is met.

⚡ Abilities — One-Time Powers (use ONCE per game)

  • The Mage 🔮 — Knows all evil players from the start. Cannot tell anyone directly.
  • Jose ⚔️ — Strike: Choose a player. If evil → eliminated. If good → Jose loses 2 cards.
  • Carl 🛡️ — Shield: Protect any player from the next ability used against them.
  • Louise 👑 — Has 3 abilities: Corrupt Draw (+2 cards to target), False Vision (see target alignment), Silence Curse (target skips ability use for 1 turn).
  • Nica 🕷️ — Hunt: Guess who the Mage is. If correct → Good team loses. If wrong → nothing.

🏆 Win Conditions

  • 🟦 Good Team Wins: Any good player empties their hand first, OR Jose eliminates Louise.
  • 🟥 Evil Team Wins: Nica correctly identifies the Mage, OR Louise's Corrupt Draw leaves a good player with 10+ cards.

👥 The Roles

🔮 The Mage — Loyal Servant of Rina
Sees all evil players from the very start. Must stay hidden. If Nica identifies them, the evil team wins instantly.
⚔️ Jose — Loyal Servant of Rina
Can eliminate one player. Kills evil = great win. Kills good = Jose loses 2 cards as punishment.
🛡️ Carl — Loyal Servant of Rina
Shields any one player from the next ability used against them. Shield expires after one use.
👑 Louise (The Malveraxis) — Shadow Court
Corrupt Draw, False Vision, Silence Curse. Three powerful one-time abilities to disrupt good players.
🕷️ Nica — Shadow Court
Hunts the Mage. If she guesses correctly once per game — the evil team wins immediately.
